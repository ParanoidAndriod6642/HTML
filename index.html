FA246: Assignment 3

In this body of work, I want to consider my creative practice within a commercial ecosystem, I want to consider this eco system because my family always say I should sell my work, and it is a direction I would like to take my work. Specifically, I want to look into markets as they are a much easier commercial entry point than stores or galleries. Applying this ethical practice of transparency involves documenting as much of the process as I can and making that easily available when looking at anything being sold. I think the easiest way to do this is do display QR codes with the products on the stall. Most importantly, I want to find a system of creation that is ethical and can be easily combined with doing what I enjoy. That will be continuing with my Ceramic, Stone, Muka and Fabric practices for the remainder of this assignment.

Lydia Tyler-Davies (2024)
